About Ling

Our Story

Ling Kitchen & Bar brings a contemporary twist to traditional Asian flavors in the heart of downtown Vancouver. We blend culinary heritage with modern techniques, creating an unforgettable dining experience.

Our bar features handcrafted cocktails inspired by Asian ingredients and flavors. Whether you're here for dinner, drinks, or late-night bites, Ling offers a vibrant atmosphere perfect for any occasion.

Philosophy

We source the finest ingredients and combine them with innovative culinary techniques. Our menu celebrates both tradition and creativity, offering dishes that are as beautiful as they are delicious.

Join us for an evening of exceptional food, creative cocktails, and unforgettable memories in Vancouver's most dynamic neighborhood.
